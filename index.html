Life and career

  • 1988â€“2013: Early Life and Rise to fame: Kiernan Jarryd Forbes also known as AKA was born in Cape Town,South Africa 28 January 1988. He is the eldest of two boys. In 2002, Forbes, alongside his two friends Vice Versa and Greyhound, formed a hip hop group called Entity The group received a nomination for a KORA Award in the Best African Hip Hop category. In 2009, he released three singles: "In My Walk", "I Do", and "Mistakes". On 28 July 2010, Forbes released "I Want It All", which is the lead single from his debut studio album, Altar Ego. The album also pre-released two singles which were "All I Know" and "Victory Lap". "Victory Lap" was successful as it topped South African local radio charts.Alter Ego was then released on 23 August 2011. The album was certified gold by RiSA for its commercial success. The album won several accolades, including the 2011 Metro FM Awards for Best Hip Hop Album. Forbes won Best Male Artist of the Year at the 2012 South African Music Awards.


  • 2014- 2022 Between 2014 and 2022 AKA released several more albums and received several more awars including but not limited to: Best Male Artist in 2012 and 2015, Street Urban Music for Alter Ego, Most Valuable Artist, Most Digital Sales, Video Of The Year For his feature on The Saga, Best Collaboration The Saga, Best Male Artist in 2012, Best Male Artist in 2015 to name but a few. AKA became a household name and gained popularity amoung fans both locally and internationally.


  • Personal life AKA and his (then) Girlfriend DJ Zinhle, welcomed the birth of their daughter Kairo Owethu Forbes, who was born in 2015. He was known to be a loving and devoted father who doted on his little princess.


  • Death On the night of the 10th of February 2023, the rapper known as AKA was gunned down infront of a restuarant in Durban and Sadley was pronounced dead on the scene.


AKA will forever be known as one of the greatest South African rappers of all time and his untimely death is a great loss to our nation. May his dear Soul Rest in peace.
